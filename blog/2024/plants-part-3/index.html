Hoya discovery

I bought a gorgeous Hoya Publicalyx from Facebook marketplace and I instantly fell in love. While I cannot find the original photos of this beautiful plant, Shortly after I bought her, I did a little photoshoot. I did meticulously document all her tendrils, baby leaves, and crazy growth patterns and I decided that I needed more hoyas!

Left: hoya publicalyx by the "E" on my shelf. Right: Elinor photoshoot with her new favorite plant!
